# Company Handbook - Rules of Engagement

## Communication Rules
- Always be polite and professional
- Reply to messages within 24 hours
- Flag any urgent messages immediately

## Financial Rules
- Flag any payment over $500 for human approval
- Never make payments without approval
- Log all transactions

## Action Rules
- Always create a Plan.md before taking action
- Sensitive actions require human approval
- Move completed tasks to /Done folder
